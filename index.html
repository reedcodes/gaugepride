Gauge Pride is an independent knit-wear provider specializing in hand-made queer knits. Each item is custom knit for the wearer, tailored to their measurements and identity.

Visit Gauge Pride on Etsy!

Gauge Pride currently offers fingerless gloves, mittens, and convertible mitts for sale. Available stripes include rainbow, bisexual, pansexual, transgender, genderqueer, and non-binary pride colors. Other color combinations are available upon request.
